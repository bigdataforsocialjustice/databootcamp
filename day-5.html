Follow these steps for Assignment 1

I am interested in the association between race/ethnicity and child maltreatment. If you recall, the Add Health data asks questions about experiences in early childhood retrospectively. That is, in Wave III, they ask respondents (who are now in their 20s) to report on experiences they had in childhood (prior to the 6th grade).

  1. Identify the child maltreatment variables in the online codebook from Wave III (see below, the topic is 'Mistreatment by Adults'). One of the questions is "By the time you started 6th grade..."

  2. Select the Race/Ethnicity variable from Wave I. After looking at the codebook, label the variables. Hint, use this variable

  3. Merge the Wave III data on mistreatment with Wave I data on race. This will be a one-to-one merge like you did in class. Export the file to a csv and import it into jamovi or jasp.

  4. Create a table of mistreatment by race/ethnicity.

  5. Choose one visualization we discussed and illustrate the relationship between mistreatment and race/ethnicity.
